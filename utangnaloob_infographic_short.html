"Utang Na Loob:
Familial Debt Cycle"

RUNTIME: 2:55

Like a piece of a bigger puzzle, "Utang Na Loob: A Debt Cycle" acts as the informational and educational sibling of "Para Sa Nanay" as it brings statistics and historical facts about the concept.

With these statitiscs, it seeks to engage the right side of the brain by breaking down the concept logically.

Open your mind and give it a watch.
