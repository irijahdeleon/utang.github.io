"Para sa Nanay"

RUNTIME: 2:30

"Para sa Nanay", is a short animated film the depicts the struggles of Filipino parents taking care of their children, by often prioritizing their health and wellness over their own.

This often leads to a mental breakdown and complete dependence on the very kids they love.

But there is a way out.
